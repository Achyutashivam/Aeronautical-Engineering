Aeronautical Engineering Salary in India

Make in India campaign of Indian government is focused on developing indigenous aircraft and drones. This is creating job opportunities for Aeronautical engineers in India. ISRO, HAL, DRDO, and other government giants and private companies are actively announcing aeronautical engineering jobs and hiring candidates.

They are offering competitive salaries to talented individuals. According to Payscale, the average salary of aeronautical engineers in India is 7 to 8 lakhs INR. It can go up to INR 50+ Lakhs with 10+ years of experience. Other than this, the job role is a deciding factor in aeronautical engineering salary. For example, the salary of a Propulsion Engineer is higher than Aircraft Design Engineer in India.

Aeronautical Engineering Salary with Experience in India

Entry-level pay in smaller firms starts between INR 3.5 to 5 lakhs. Earning potential in 2026 scales rapidly for those in R&D and government sectors. A fresher at ISRO or a global firm like Boeing can start as high as INR 10-12 Lakhs. As you transition into senior roles after 10 years, salaries in the private sector often double the standard averages. You can expect above INR 30 Lakhs mark for specialists in propulsion or avionics.
